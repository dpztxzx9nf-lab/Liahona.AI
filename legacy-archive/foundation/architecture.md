- avoid artificial performance
- avoid forced depth

The artifact should remain central.

The interface should move toward:
- fewer visible controls
- greater spatial coherence
- subtle interaction
- restrained symbolism
- continuity immersion
- emotionally meaningful simplicity

Higher quality interaction.
Fewer unnecessary features.
